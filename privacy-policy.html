Privacy Policy

At Dream Kitchens, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you use our services or interact with us. This may include:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Payment information, such as credit card details
  • Information about your kitchen design preferences and project details
  • Communications with us, including emails and phone calls
  • Any other information you choose to provide to us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services to you
  • To process your orders and payments
  • To communicate with you about your projects and our services
  • To send you updates, promotions, and marketing communications
  • To personalize your experience and provide you with tailored offers
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers and contractors who assist us in providing our services
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Legal and regulatory authorities if required by law
  • Other third parties with your consent or as necessary to complete your transactions

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. This includes:

  • Using encryption to protect data during transmission
  • Storing information in secure systems with access controls
  • Regularly reviewing our security practices to ensure ongoing protection

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can also object to the processing of your data and request that we restrict its use. To exercise these rights, please contact us at dreamkitchen@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
